Lasagna

Best Meat Lasagna in the World

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatos
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings (fennel seeds, black pepper and salt
  • Lasgna Noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella and ricota
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
